Privacy Policy

Last updated: April, 2025 · VEPA AutoCare, Albany NY

This policy explains what information VEPA AutoCare collects, how we use it, and your rights. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers.

1. Who We Are

VEPA AutoCare is a family-owned auto repair shop and used vehicle dealership located at 1904 Western Ave, Albany, NY 12203. You can reach us at contact@vepaautocare.com or (518) 456-5682.

2. Information We Collect

Account information — Name, email, and phone number (when you contact us or book services)

Appointment data — Vehicle details (for service appointments)

Contact form submissions — Messages you send through our contact forms

Usage data — Basic website usage data (such as page visits)

Cookies — We use essential cookies required for authentication

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To schedule and manage service appointments
  • To send appointment confirmation and reminder emails
  • To respond to contact form messages
  • To improve the website and our services
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for advertising profiling, third-party marketing, or any automated decision-making that produces legal effects.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may use trusted third-party services (such as Google) to help operate our website and manage appointments. These providers handle data securely under their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your experience and understand how the site is used. By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@vepaautocare.com. We will respond as soon as possible.

8. Security

Our website uses secure connections (HTTPS). We take reasonable measures to protect your information and keep it secure.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out:

  • Email: contact@vepaautocare.com
  • Phone: (518) 456-5682
  • Address: 1904 Western Ave, Albany, NY 12203
