How It Started

Our Story

Bought and rebranded in 2022, VEPA AutoCare is a family-owned automotive shop that has been proudly serving the Albany community from day one.

We set out with a simple goal: create a shop where customers feel respected, not rushed. Where every estimate is explained clearly and every repair is done right the first time.

What started as a vision for better local auto service has grown into a shop that families trust for everything from routine oil changes to complex engine repairs. We're proud to call Albany home.

What Drives Us

Our Values

Honesty First

We tell you exactly what your car needs and nothing more, nothing less. No upsells, no scare tactics.

Transparency

Community

We're your neighbors. We live here too, and we take pride in taking care of our community's vehicles.
